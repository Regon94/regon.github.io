The Pale Blue Dot Index

The pale blue dot index aims to capture efficiency in global GDP growth. It does so by broadening the definition of "growth". At the moment when we hear about global growth or national growth the measure includes only economic growth. This index aims to include more factors, what is the diversity of flora and fauna in the country, how many eco-systems has it kept alive, how many new eco systems does it have access to?(space and sea).
