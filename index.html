Hi, I'm Roger.

Welcome to this online space where I capture some ideas and tools.

I am an aerospace engineer passionate about the application of systems thinking. I like to refer to systems thinking as the meta-mental model as all mental models begin by viewing the subject as a system. My main objective is to build things which enable everyone to solve the increasingly complex problems of today.


I am interested in many topics including decision making models, mathematical modelling, electric aviation, space propulsion, the optimisation between performance and longevity and the dynamics of networks. Hopefully through writing on this website we will begin to narrow down and focus on fewer topics. I also try to capture moments with color and words through acrylics and poetry.
My personal objectives are to build rich relationships, stay physically fit for as long as possible and build meaningful things.
