Barefaced

The incessant pull of the horizon,
like gravity to the mind
eliminates the boundaries of thought in my kind
through these new possibilities we become again

The everlasting requires no myth
though great descriptions have been writ
it's the silence that draws me closer
An undisturbed pool with an edge of light
The son will silence the waves tonight
tomorrow the symphony begins again
another step forward, no less remain
